Data Protection (GDPR)

This website does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics. The contact form collects an email address and comments solely to respond to user enquiries. This constitutes “legitimate interest” processing under UK GDPR. No data is stored on a server; the form uses mailto: which opens the user’s email client directly. Personal data (committee member names) is published with consent and limited to names and roles only—no email addresses or phone numbers are displayed.

Security Considerations

The contact form uses mailto:, meaning data is transmitted via the user’s own email client rather than a server-side script. This avoids risks associated with server-side data storage and injection attacks. In a production environment, a secure server-side handler with HTTPS, input validation, and CSRF protection would be recommended. All external links use rel="noopener noreferrer" to prevent reverse tabnabbing.
