What do we do?

We are committed to fostering collaboration and communication between students, staff, and administrators. Our meetings bring together senior members of staff, administration staff, and lecturers with Class Reps to discuss subject-wide or School-wide issues.

About the Department

The department, primarily housed in Regent Court, plays a pivotal role in providing an enriching educational experience. Regent Court comprises two separate buildings built around a central courtyard with internal access between them. Our dedicated staff is focused on creating a conducive learning environment for students.

Contacting the Department:

It is vital to keep your contact details up-to-date for routine matters and emergencies. Feel free to contact us for any queries or concerns.

Finding Academic Staff:

Most staff members are located in Regent Court, but some offices are in other buildings on campus. We recommend arranging appointments with staff before visiting their offices.

Explore more about the department on the official department website.

University Information about Staff-Student Committees

Our committee is a valuable service that addresses a wide range of student queries. We provide guidance on issues such as exams, changing courses, and extenuating circumstances. Feel free to reach out to us for support.
